Welcome to Eldoris Team.

We are a Subs in Schools team from The Heights School competing in the 2020 Subs in Schools challenge. We will be posting our details and regular updates of our rov on this website feel free to check out what we do.

What Do We Do?

We are making a Remote operated Vehicle (Rov) which is usually used in deep sea exploration. To do this we are a team of six we have the main engineers, marketing team and the production line. Each of these lines play a key role in making the Rov. We must ensure to work as a team and collaborate.
